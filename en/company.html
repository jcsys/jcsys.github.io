Message from Top Management

Since the establishment of the company in 2002, J.C.System has taken the two pillars of software development of embedded systems such as business department, mobile phone, automobile and BSE service business for offshore development as the main business.


"There are far fewer offshore successes than failures. China's high labor costs are no longer tenable. Overseas development is the result of hard work, and as a result, the cost will become higher… “Recently, for offshore development, the voice of difficulties is getting louder and louder.

 

The company's BSE business is made up of Chinese SE who have been in Japan for more than 10 years and have extensive experience in design and management.

 

Led by the large-scale mobile phone development in the future mobile phone era, it is now centered on the development of vehicle and car navigation system, and has participated in the offshore development of various scales.
Japanese guests + the company's professional BSE+ offshore team system, has achieved a lot of successful results.
Now the speed of social change has accelerated, and the trend of the times has changed greatly, from "competition" to "co-creation", that is, from simple offshore to the sharing of resources and the division of labor and cooperation of technology.

 

 In addition, although the company is a small number of elite establishment, but with circulation and vehicle systems as the core technology, active use of overseas rich resources, product undertakings, outsourcing, global development, etc., often introduce new technology trends, in response to the requirements of the times.

 

President　Takaishi Syusei
